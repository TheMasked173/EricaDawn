ERICA DAWN BOOKS

Mollie’s Ancestral Gift is a gentle folklore fantasy rooted in magical realism, serving as a love letter to heritage. Guided by Aunt Grace, Mollie discovers through whispered memories, ancestral echoes, and the quiet magic of a lakeside castle, that her family’s legacy lives within her. Destined to become Keeperess of family memories, she must honor the past while carrying its wisdom forward.

About the Author

EricaDawn writes stories where folklore whispers and magic dances through every page. In her debut middle-grade fantasy, Mollie’s Ancestral Gift, she writes a love letter to heritage. When EricaDawn isn’t conjuring stories, she lives by a shimmering lake with her three spirited boys, listening to their melodies float from school band performances. She treasures adventures with friends, whether across distant lands or in the quiet magic of home.

By day, she leads as an executive in a lending company; by heart, she is a dreamer shaped by her journeys through Georgetown University and Baylor Law School. Her stories draw life from her devotion to her ancestors and the rich history they left as a gift to the future. She publishes her stories to inspire her boys and bonus children.
